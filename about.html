Our Project

As we explore the city, we'll encounter bustling markets, tranquil gardens, and lively festivals. We'll listen to the sounds of street vendors selling spicy noodles, children laughing and playing games, and musicians performing traditional folk songs. The urban soundscape will be a symphony of car horns, bicycle bells, and the hum of conversation in various languages.

But it's not just about the sounds we hear. We'll also delve into the stories behind them, discovering the history and traditions that have shaped the city into what it is today. We'll learn about the significance of certain sounds and how they have become woven into the fabric of the culture.

Through this journey, we'll gain a deeper appreciation for the city and its people. We'll come to understand how their unique experiences have influenced their way of life and how they view the world. And perhaps, along the way, we'll discover a few stories of our own. So put on your headphones, open your ears and let’s embark on this sonic adventure!

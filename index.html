Sounds?

Have you ever dive into specific sounds of a city?

From the rhythmic hum of traffic to the chatter of people on the street. The gentle lapping of waves at the beach or even the sounds of birds singing or leaves rustling in the wind. Each city has its own distinctive soundscape that transports us to a different time and place.

Stories

A city is a place where different stories unfold every day, where the lives of people from different walks of life intersect and weave together in a tapestry of human experience. Each street corner, each building, each park holds its own secrets, its own history, its own characters. And within this vibrant tapestry, lies a myriad of stories waiting to be told.

Hangzhou

Story of Nainai and her relation with her hometown, Hangzhou, through the recollection of her grandson.

"As I grew older, I began to understand just how deep Nainai's love for Hangzhou ran. It wasn't just about the physical beauty of the city or the memories she had there. It was about the sense of community and belonging she felt when she was there. Despite the distance, Hangzhou would always hold a special place in her heart."

Qingdao

A love poem from a visitor of Qingdao to the guy who had lit up her life in its darkest time.

"You came into my world like a beacon of light, illuminating the darkness that had taken over me. The ocean waves crash against the shore, reminding me of the ebb and flow of our love. Through the ups and downs, the highs and lows, you have remained my constant.Your smile is like the sunrise - Bringing light to my darkest skies - Your laughter is like music to my ears - Washing away all my fears."

Rizhao

A girl revisits the haunts of her youth after a decade of escaping from the grief brought on by her brother's death.

"No matter how far she went or how hard she tried to forget, the memories and the pain always followed her.As the sun begins to set, the girl stands up and wipes away her tears. She knows that it's time to leave, but she also knows that she will come back. For the first time in a long time, she felt free from the weight of her grief"

Shanghai

A busy city where all people pay their blood, sweat, and tears for a promising future.

" It's a city where the rhythm of life is set to the relentless beat of ambition, a place where dreams are woven from the threads of hard work and determination. This is a story of Shanghai, where the currency of choice is not dollars or cents, but rather, blood, sweat, and tears, each drop a testament to the unyielding spirit of those who call it home."

A Million Stories in One City

Get ready to listen to a million tales in one vibrant city! “A Million Stories in One City” is a series of sonic narratives that showcase Chinese cities' rich culture and lifestyle through sounds. With each step we take, we'll immerse ourselves in the local communities, learn from the locals, and draw inspiration from their rich heritage. Join us on this sonic adventure and let's soak up all the rich heritage that surrounds us!
